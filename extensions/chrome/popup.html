NorseCourse Scheduler
  1. Find A Course and Plan A Schedule
  2. Confirm Schedule Preferences
  3. Search for Schedules
  4. Browse through Results and save your favorite schedules
  5. Select the schedule you want to register by navigating to it in Saved Schedules
  6. Switch to the Register tab in this extension
  7. Select 'Retrieve Schedule'
  8. Navigate to my.luther.edu > Register for Sections > Search and register for sections
  9. Select 'Register Schedule'
  10. Submit, Select Courses, and Register!
